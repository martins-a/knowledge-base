## Machine Learning

- Supervised Learning:
The model is trained on labeled data, where each input has a known output. It learns to map inputs to the correct outputs based on these examples.

- Unsupervised Learning:
The model is trained on unlabeled data. It discovers patterns or structures on its own, often by grouping similar data points into clusters.

- Reinforcement Learning:
The model learns by interacting with an environment and receiving feedback (rewards or penalties). Over time, it improves its decisions by learning from its mistakes and successes.
